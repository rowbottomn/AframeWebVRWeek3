AFrame Entities and Components

Previously we have worked with a lot of tags that were created for us.

  • We used some for shapes like a-box and a-sphere.
  • We also used some that are built in to AFrame like a-sky and a-scene.
  • We lastly used some that were made for custom components taken from the AFrame registry like a-animation.
Our goal is to make our own tags of a sort.
